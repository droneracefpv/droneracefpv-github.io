How It Works

1. Login

Login to our platform on your computer.

2. Choose a Race

Choose one of our drone race tracks and pick a race. Races usually last about 5-10 minutes.

3. Fly

Once all opponents are ready, our platform will log you in remotely to one of our drones, at which point the race starts and you have full control over our drone. Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the drone to the finish line.

Technology

All of our race drones are equipped with an independent flight controller to manage the flight, as well as an on-board computer with a camera to provide autopilot navigation and vision. There are geofences in place to restrict the drone's movement to the race track. Additionally, all the drones are equipped with sensors that prevent crashing into walls and objects. You will not be able to crash them. Try it.
